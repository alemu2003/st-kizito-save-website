ABOUT US



IN FACT
St kizito is located at save.it is Few miles North of the Town Huye and in West of Gisagara District of Southern Province Rwanda .before 1962,saint Kizito save TVET school was an orphanage for children mullattoes of save running by whit sister.from 1962 to 1972, the instition become junior seminary for catholic father to be in 1972,the former bishop of butare, his lordship Jean baptiste GAHAMANYI decided to shift the seminary to butare (karubanda),

SAINT KIZITO SAVE T.S.S is a rural boarding school set on one of Rwanda’s thousand hills, the capital of Rwanda a country that many believe to be the future development model for all of Africa.
This vocational school trains up to1025 motivated boys and girls, training the skills necessary to find a job or progress to a university on a government scholarship. Specialties include Civil construction,software development,electronicsand service,multimedia,and industrial Electricity And Other trades listed below.

EXCITING PRIORITIES
Our new educational mission is to become a top trade school in Rwanda, with an emphasis on English proficiency and “self-learning,” so that our trainees can self-adapt to a rapidly modernizing Rwandan and African economy. We are currently rated by the Rwandan Government to be out of 97 technical secondary schools in Rwanda. Our next goal is to be in the top one.
FABULOUS OPPORTUNITY
To Educate For The Future It takes a team to build a great school. The base is already there: a competent teaching staff, a motivated administration, and an impressive physical plant.
SAINT KIZITO SAVE T.S.S mission is to support Rwanda’s growth and to continue vision in one of the poorest areas of Rwanda, by educating young people in the skills necessary for 100% of them to find a job or enroll in a university.
IMPORTANT TRAINING INITIATION
SAINT KIZITO SAVE T.S.S administration has implemented a significant new education initiative, designed to catapult the school into the ranks of the three top professional schools in Rwanda. We are quite proud that trainees enrollment at SAINT KIZITO SAVE T.S.S.
IMPROVEMENT OF ENGLISH SKILLS
A keypriority for economic development in Rwanda is the continuing transition from French to English speaking. It is also a key priority for ETSK that is now being supported by a number of important initiatives.
TRAINING FOR JOB
SAINT KIZITO SAVE T.S.S educates students in the three professions that offer the most opportunity for employment in Rwanda, Africa: Builiding Construction,Software development,elecronics and industial Electricity . Each course follows strict government education guidelines, with a new emphasis on practical skills and work internships.
SPORTS
sports and athletics are important education activities, building character, teamwork, and cooperation. The school provides excellent sports facilities with daily sports schedules for the following: football (soccer), volleyball, basketball, and handball. We have competitive men’s teams in both soccer and basketball, and a special Zumba exercise class for the growing number of women at SAINT KIZITO SAVE T.S.S (now about 1/3 of our student body).
