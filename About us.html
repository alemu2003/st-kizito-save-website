"Skills for a better destiny"

    • Saint kizito is located at save.it is Few miles North of the Town Huye and in West of Gisagara District of Southern Province Rwanda .,

    it is place characterised by dwelling,several school,church ,Health centre and other facilitiesoperated by Religious Group for the purposeof providing charitable sevice and propagate religion.

    before 1962,saint Kizito save TVET school was an orphanage for children mullattoes of save running by whit sister.

    from 1962 to 1972, the instition become junior seminary for catholic father to be in 1972,the former bishop of butare, his lordship Jean baptiste GAHAMANYI decided to shift the seminary to butare (karubanda).

    At the same period the bishop invited van dale Brothers to open the centre pof technicians.

    According to the official letter wrote by the ministry of education on 22nd of december,1972,the centre opened and received the first student on 2nd of january,1973 at Save.
